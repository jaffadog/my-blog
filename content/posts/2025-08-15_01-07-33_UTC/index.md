It’s another episode #FAD buoy tear downs - with a happy ending (converted to solar lantern). This is a 🇪🇸 #marineinstruments #M3iGo I found washed up on the reef here in #Katiu. Ok looking at the innards: nicely constructed solar solar array elevated for better thermal performance. Single board. #Iridium #SBD module. #Ublox #GPS module. Each with separate ceramic patch antennas on the flip side of the board. Strangle collection of 3 battery packs. 2 alkaline. 1 NiMH. Orientation sensor of some sort. Transducer. Ok, I wanted to modify this so that the LEDs remain on full time - so that I can donate it for copra shack lighting. I chose to bypass the switching transistor with a short yellow jumper cable. The jumper is soldered on an available GND pad and the #45 test pad. Reassemble and we’re good to go 👍☀️💡
