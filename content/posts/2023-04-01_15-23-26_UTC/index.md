Tacking past HMS Diamond Rock on our way to Saint Anne, Martinique. The rock, which is just a stone throw from Martinique, was fortified by the Brits, equipped with cannon, operated as a "stone frigate", and saw naval engagements in the early 1800s. Crazy stuff! #calypsosailsagain #martinique
